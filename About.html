About the Degree

Students need to meet the Degree Requirements below to earn this Bachelor of Science degree in LAS.


Hours   Requirements
27 Required Computer Science Courses
3 CS 125 - Introduction to Computer Science
3 CS 173 - Discrete Structures
3 CS 225 - Data Structure
4 CS 233 - Computer Architecture
4 CS 241 - Systems Programming
3 CS 242 - Programming Studio
4 CS 374 - Algorithms and Models of Computation
(Currently CS 498B)
3 CS 421 - Programming Languages & Compilers
12 Mathematics (may also fulfill the General Education Quantitative Reasoning I and II requirements)
3 MATH 221 - Calculus I
3 MATH 225 - Introductory Matrix Theory I
3 MATH 231 - Calculus II
3 STAT 100 - Statistics
Minimum of 24 Hours Linguistics Coursework
An additional 24 hours of coursework in Linguistics. Coursework must be chosen in consultation with and approved by the Linguistics advisor (Professor Girju). Must include at least 12 hours at the 300- or 400-level.
15 Required linguistics courses
3 LING 100 - Intro to Language Sciences
3 LING 301- Elements of Syntax
3 LING 307 - Elements of Semantics and Pragmatics
3 LING 401 - Intro to General Phonetics
3 LING 406 - Intro to Computational Linguistics
6 Two of the following:
3 LING 490 (63730) - Seminar in Linguistic Analysis (Corpus Linguistics)
3 LING 490 (62082) - Seminar in Linguistic Analysis (Human and Machine Translation: History and Applications)
4 LING 490 (65072) - Seminar in Linguistic Analysis (Tools for Big Data)
3 LING 490 (56284) - Seminar in Linguistic Analysis (Preparing for Career Success)
3 One of the following:
3 LING 270 - Language, Technology and Society
3 LING 302 - Elements of Phonology

1Twelve hours of 300- and 400-level courses in the major must be taken on this campus.

All LAS foreign language requirements must be satisfied.

A Major Plan of Study Form must be completed and submitted to the LAS Student Affairs Office by the beginning of the fifth semester (60-75 hours). Please see a Computer Science advisor as well as the advisor in Linguistics (Professor Girju).

Note that the above is subject to change as the program continues to develop. Contact Professor Girju for the most up-to-date information.
