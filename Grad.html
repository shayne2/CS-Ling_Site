Graduate Programs


It is never too early to start looking into Graduate School. Many unviersities in the U.S. and abroad allow you to study Computational Linguistics/Natural Language Processing while work towards either a Master's or Ph.D. in such areas as Computer Science, Linguistics, Information Science, Library and Information Science, Language Technology, etc.
